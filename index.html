Meet Jace Frenkel

Founder & Team Leader, Frenkel Financial Agency

I started Frenkel Financial Agency to build the kind of team I wish I'd had when I got into this industry — one where new agents get real mentorship instead of being handed a script and left to figure it out alone.

My focus is simple: help our agents protect families with the right life insurance products, give them the training and tools to do that confidently, and create a clear path to grow their income and, eventually, lead their own team.

If you're licensed (or working toward it) and looking for a team that actually invests in you, I'd love to talk.

What You Get When You Join the Team

We built Frenkel Financial Agency to fix the things that make most agencies frustrating to work for. Here's what that looks like day to day.

Real Mentorship

Get hands-on training from day one — not just a manual, but ongoing support from people who've done the job.

Transparent Commissions

Clear contract levels from 80%–145% with a defined path to increase your comp as you grow with the agency.

Free CRM Access

Every Frenkel Financial agent gets free access to ProducerStack — the CRM built and run by our CEO, Jace Frenkel — for managing leads, policies, and commissions. The same platform costs general-public users over $150/month.

Licensing Support

Not licensed yet? We'll point you toward the right resources and help you get appointed once you are.

Path to Leadership

As you grow, so does your opportunity — build and lead your own team within the agency.

In-House Leads

New agents get free leads supplied directly by the agency — a great way to practice, build confidence, and start generating revenue without spending your own money on lead generation.

Remote & In-Person Roles

Most positions are fully remote, so you can work from anywhere. We also have in-person opportunities at our Madison and Appleton, Wisconsin offices for agents who want to work alongside the team.

Carrier & Agency Bonuses

Earn producer bonuses paid directly by the insurance carriers based on your production, plus additional incentive bonuses from Frenkel Financial Agency for hitting growth milestones.
